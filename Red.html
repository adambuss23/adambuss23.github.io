-Internship Photo Journal-

A visual journal of our time in philly interning at Red Tettemer. The photos were taken on a nishika n8000 film camera then overlaid to create the moving gif effect. The process of learning to use this camera went hand in hand with the trial and error of working at our internship. It took numerous rolls to get anything usable but made it so much more rewarding in the end. I then took the pictures and coded a site for them to live on rather than make the traditional photo book.
