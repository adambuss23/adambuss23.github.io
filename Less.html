-LESS APP-

Less is a minimalist app prototype that was created to help the user determine the things they could get rid of from their life. Using Bluetooth beacons the app would log and keep track of the things the user interacted with daily. I started the design by working out a process flow for how the app would function. Then I wireframed and user tested the app. Using the things learned from user testing I created a revised process and made it functional with invision. VIEW THE FULL PROCESS WRITEUP HERE
